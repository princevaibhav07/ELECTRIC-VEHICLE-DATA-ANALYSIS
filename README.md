# Electric Vehicle Data Analysis Dashboard

This repository contains a Tableau dashboard project that provides a comprehensive analysis of electric vehicle (EV) data across the United States. The dashboard explores various aspects of EV adoption, distribution, and trends from 2010 onwards, offering insights for manufacturers, policymakers, and consumers.

The Electric Vehicle Data Analysis Dashboard includes multiple views and visualizations, each designed to answer key questions about the EV landscape.

### Key Visualizations

1. **Total Vehicles by Model Year**:
   - **Visualization**: Line/Area Chart
   - **Description**: Tracks the growth and adoption trends of electric vehicles from 2010 onwards.

2. **Total Vehicles by State**:
   - **Visualization**: Map Chart
   - **Description**: Displays the geographic distribution of EVs, allowing users to identify states with higher EV adoption.

3. **Top 10 Vehicles by Make**:
   - **Visualization**: Bar Chart
   - **Description**: Highlights the top 10 EV manufacturers, showing market dominance and popular brands.

4. **Total Vehicles by CAFV Eligibility**:
   - **Visualization**: Donut Chart
   - **Description**: Shows the proportion of EVs eligible for Clean Alternative Fuel Vehicle (CAFV) incentives, helping assess the role of incentives in adoption.

5. **Top 10 Vehicles by Model**:
   - **Visualization**: Tree Map
   - **Description**: Lists the top 10 EV models based on vehicle count, providing insights into consumer preferences.

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- **Total Vehicles**: Shows the overall number of EVs, including both Battery Electric Vehicles (BEVs) and Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEVs).
- **Average Electric Range**: Provides the average range of EVs, indicating technological advancements and efficiency.
- **Total BEV Vehicles and % of Total BEV Vehicles**: Analyzes the total number of BEVs and their market share, showcasing the popularity of fully electric models.
- **Total PHEV Vehicles and % of Total PHEV Vehicles**: Analyzes the total number of PHEVs and their market share, highlighting the role of plug-in hybrids.
